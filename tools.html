About Handyman

Handyman is a modern toolkit for the web, designed with your privacy in mind. It's all about making your work easier and faster. With Handyman, you get a bunch of tools that are made just for developers like you.

Every tool in Handyman is made to fit your needs. Whether you're a beginner just starting out or a seasoned pro, there's something here for you. And because it's made to be simple and easy to use, you'll be up and running in no time.

Contribution

We actively encourage community contributions. Whether it involves bug fixes, documentation enhancements, or feature additions, your input is invaluable. Please refer to our GitHub repository for detailed contribution guidelines.
